Hamed Mozaffari

Robotics • Control • Estimation • Sensor Fusion

I am a dual-background Ph.D. researcher in Robotics and Control Engineering with deep experience in state estimation, nonlinear control, human–robot interaction, 6-DOF manipulation, and intelligent autonomy. I develop modern estimation frameworks, delay-aware Bayesian filters, robot learning algorithms, and embedded control systems for real-world robotics and autonomous platforms.

My work spans both academia and industry, combining analytical modeling with hands-on implementation in ROS2, C++, Python, MATLAB, and embedded systems. I focus on designing reliable robotic systems that integrate perception, planning, control, and human-centered autonomy.
